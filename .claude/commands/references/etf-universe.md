## Cost Benchmark

**Target weighted TER: < 0.30%** (revised from 0.20% to account for Avantis factor tilt premium)

The ~0.10–0.15% TER premium over passive index funds is justified by the Fama-French research showing 1–3% expected annual factor premium from value + profitability tilts. Any single ETF with TER > 0.50% should be reviewed.
