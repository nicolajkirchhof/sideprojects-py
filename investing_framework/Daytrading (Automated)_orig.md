# Daytrading

## Global rules

- Trades against the trend defined by 20EMA are only allowed on strong S/R levels (prior day/week high/low/close) with SL that is moved to BE as soon as possible
- Scalping is only allowed in direction of the trend
- Same trade idea can only executed twice
- Entries can only be done after a potential entry bar has fully developed. Entries based on developing bars are strictly forbidden.

## S/R Swing Reasons for Entry

- At last two medium sized bars (or one large) close fully above last candle in opposite direction
- The two bars have almost no overlay and only little wigs
- At best there are many bars invalidated by these counter trend bars
- Wigs of the candles are larger on the counter trend direction than on the trend direction
- The reversal happens at one of the key levels: ONH, ONL, PDH, PDL, PDC, PWH, PWL
- The overlaying trend (Gap, Overnight range, Prior Days) supports the direction
- There was a previous trend change a close price level (double top/bottom)
- Get in when the top bar is overlapped by at least one large or two medium sized reversal bars in counter trend direction.
- Wait for the bars to fully form

## S/R Swing SL placement

- Move the stop loss with every major S/R area you encounter.
- These are typically PB and breakouts in trend direction
- Usually below the last low in bull trend and last high in bear trend.
- Do not just move to BE always use a reasonable S/R area/level

## S/R Swing Scale in

- Wait for a new entry signal after a pullback
- If you are sure that a losing trade has the premise to get back to the entry level, Scale in at 50% PB and place limit order on BE of first entry.

## S/R Swing Reasons for Exit

- Doji forms with large wig in trend direction
- Strong signal with following counter trend bar (wait for bar to fully form)
- Consecutive candles with large wigs in trend direction are forming
- The body of the candles or the overall ATR gets smaller
- There are counter trend candle forming
- Consecutive candles overlap the prior candle low/high
- VWAP forms a top/bottom
- 20 EMA gets neutral
- Doji @ double top / bottom
- Two times the actual or initial risk is reached

## Scalp

- Setup should be a defined trading range
- Entry at the low of the candle for long
- Move SL to BE on half of candle height in your favor
- Tight SL just below the range
- Take profit at the high end of the candle/range (approx.)

## Mean reversion on News/Earnings

- Wait until a strong push/pull fades after a news event has settled
- Get in with a loose SL and try to catch some of the reversal
- Apply a trail SL when possible to ride the reversal systematically
